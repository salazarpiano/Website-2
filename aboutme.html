About Me
Batons

I am an aspiring young conductor, pianist, and music educator based in Los Angeles, California. In January 2018 I was awarded the very first Conducting Fellowship at the Santa Clarita Valley Youth Orchestra, and I now hold the position of Assistant Conductor. At CalArts, I am an in-demand conductor, who often leads student compositions, and student ensembles. I am well versed in not only classical works, but also modern compositions. I was awarded a full scholarship to study at the CalArts Piano department with Dr. Vicki Ray, and I have performed numerous times as both solo pianist and chamber player. I am proficient in harpsichord continuo playing, and also have composed numerous works for various ensemble types. In 2014 I was a finalist in the Southern California Junior Bach Festival, where I received Regional Honors. I started playing piano at 6 years old, and decided to pursue music as a career at 14 years old. My life goal is to become the conductor of a professional orchestra, and teach as a professor of conducting and piano.
